## **TROUBLESHOOTING**

### **If GitHub upload is slow:**
- This is normal for large projects
- Wait patiently (can be 5-10 min total)
- Do not refresh page

### **If Vercel deployment fails:**
- Check Vercel "Deployments" tab
- Click "Redeploy" button
- Wait again (usually works second time)

### **If you can't access GitHub/Vercel:**
- Company firewall may block it
- Contact your IT department
- Ask for access to github.com and vercel.com
- Or ask a colleague to deploy for you

### **If app doesn't load after deploy:**
- Wait 1-2 more minutes
- Refresh browser (Ctrl+Shift+R hard refresh)
- Check Vercel logs for errors
